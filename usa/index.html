Blind maps

A very simple that helps you realize where countrie, states, counties, cities or rivers actually are in the world.

How to play

Once you hit the button below, we'll pull the trigger and the stopwatch will start counting. That's it. Simple, isn't it?

Remember

  • Yellow is what you're asked about
  • Red means you were wrong
  • Green means you were right
  • Once you're finished, view right answers by hovering your mouse over the map
